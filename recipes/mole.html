Mole

Description

Typical nahutal stew.

If you are able to find the main ingredient you are set.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Doña maría mole sauce (or any other brand if you can find it)
  • 3 Chiles anchos (sometimes called poblanos as well)
  • Half a dark chocolate bar
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Chop the chicken into small pieces and fry it.
  2. Blend the mole sauce briefly.
  3. Place the chiles in hot water (not boiling) for a couple of minutes.
  4. Place the chiles in the blender with the mole and blend till there are no visible chunks left.
  5. Add chocolate to the mix and sugar till it tastes right, you can also use nesquik if you don't have any chocolate bars handy.
  6. Add the mix to the chicken and let it sit on medium fire until it has the right thickness.
