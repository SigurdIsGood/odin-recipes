Chile colorado

Description

This dish constitues around 80% of your average mexican diet.

Finding the right kind of chile is the key.

Ingredients

  • Beef or pork
  • Chile de la tierra (sometimes called guajillo)
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • A single cube of chicken bouillon
  • (Optional) Maize flour

Steps

  1. Chop your meat of choice into small chunks.
  2. Cook the meat in a pan (whichever doneness you prefer is fine)
  3. Boil the chiles.
  4. Put the chiles with some water in the blender along the oregano, garlic and bouillon
  5. (Optional) Mix the flour with some water and salt and make small spheres with the mix
  6. Add the chile mix to the meat and let it rest on medium fire
  7. If you decided to add the flour balls add them while the stew is cooking on medium fire
